LOANS & CREDIT PRODUCTS

PERSONAL LOANS
Loan amounts: $1,000 – $50,000
Interest rates: 7.99% – 24.99% APR (based on credit score and income)
Repayment terms: 12, 24, 36, 48, or 60 months
Funds disbursed within 1-2 business days after approval.

Eligibility requirements:
- Minimum credit score: 620
- Minimum annual income: $25,000
- Active account holder for at least 90 days

To apply: mobile app → Products → Personal Loan, or visit any branch.

HOME LOANS / MORTGAGES
Fixed-rate mortgages: 15-year and 30-year terms available.
Adjustable-rate mortgages (ARM): 5/1, 7/1, and 10/1 ARM options.
Minimum down payment: 3% for conforming loans, 20% to avoid PMI.
Pre-approval takes 1-3 business days. Full approval takes 30-45 days.

AUTO LOANS
Loan amounts: $5,000 – $100,000
Rates from 4.99% APR for new vehicles, 6.99% APR for used vehicles.
Terms: 24 – 84 months.

CREDIT CARDS
Available cards:
- Basic Checking Card: No annual fee, 1% cashback on all purchases
- Rewards Plus Card: $95 annual fee, 3% cashback on dining/travel, 1.5% on everything else
- Secured Card: For building credit, requires a $200–$5,000 security deposit

Credit limit increases can be requested after 6 months of on-time payments.

CREDIT SCORE MONITORING
Free FICO score available monthly in the mobile app under: Profile → Credit Score.
