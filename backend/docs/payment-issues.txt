PAYMENT ISSUES — TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

DECLINED TRANSACTIONS
A transaction may be declined for the following reasons:
- Insufficient funds in the account
- Transaction exceeds daily spending limit
- Card reported lost or stolen
- Suspicious activity detected (fraud hold)
- Incorrect PIN entered 3 times (card temporarily locked)

To resolve a declined transaction:
1. Check your account balance in the mobile app or online banking
2. Verify your daily spending limit under Settings → Card Controls
3. If you believe fraud is involved, call 1-800-BANK-HELP immediately
4. To unlock a PIN-locked card, visit any branch or call support

DAILY LIMITS
- Debit card purchases: $5,000 per day
- ATM withdrawals: $1,000 per day
- Peer-to-peer transfers (Zelle, etc.): $2,500 per day
- Wire transfers: $25,000 per day (requires additional verification)

To request a temporary limit increase, call customer support at least 24 hours in advance.

INTERNATIONAL PAYMENTS
International transactions are subject to a 3% foreign transaction fee.
To avoid declined cards abroad: notify the bank at least 48 hours before travel via the mobile app under Settings → Travel Notice.

FAILED BANK TRANSFERS
ACH transfers typically take 1-3 business days.
If a transfer fails, funds are returned within 2 business days.
Common causes: incorrect routing/account number, closed account, insufficient funds.
