REFUND POLICY

Customers can request a refund within 30 days of a transaction.
Refunds are processed within 5 business days and returned to the original payment method.
For refunds on debit card transactions, the amount is credited back to the linked bank account.
For credit card transactions, the refund appears as a statement credit within 1-2 billing cycles.

Non-refundable items:
- Wire transfer fees
- Foreign transaction fees
- Monthly account maintenance fees
- Overdraft protection fees

To request a refund, customers can:
1. Use the mobile app: Go to Transactions → select the transaction → tap "Request Refund"
2. Call customer support at 1-800-BANK-HELP (available 24/7)
3. Visit any branch with a valid government-issued photo ID

Refund requests submitted after 30 days require manager approval and are evaluated case-by-case.
