ACCOUNT ACCESS & RESET GUIDE

PASSWORD RESET
To reset your online banking password:
1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for a reset link (valid for 15 minutes)
4. Create a new password — minimum 8 characters, must include one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character

If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or call 1-800-BANK-HELP.

LOCKED ACCOUNT
Your account is locked after 5 consecutive failed login attempts.
To unlock: wait 30 minutes for automatic unlock, or call customer support for immediate assistance.

TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (2FA)
2FA is required for all online banking logins.
Supported methods: SMS OTP, email OTP, authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy).
To change your 2FA method: Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication.

LOST OR STOLEN CARD
Report a lost or stolen card immediately:
- Mobile app: Cards → Report Lost/Stolen (instant freeze)
- Phone: 1-800-BANK-HELP (24/7)
A replacement card is issued within 5-7 business days. Expedited delivery (1-2 days) available for $15.

CLOSING AN ACCOUNT
To close a bank account:
1. Ensure the account balance is zero or transfer remaining funds
2. Cancel any automatic payments or direct deposits linked to the account
3. Submit a closure request in writing at any branch or via certified mail
Accounts with a negative balance cannot be closed until the balance is cleared.

UPDATING PERSONAL INFORMATION
To update address, phone number, or email: log in → Profile → Personal Information.
Name changes require a legal document (marriage certificate, court order) submitted at a branch.
